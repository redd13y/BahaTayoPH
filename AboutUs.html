We Are BahaTayo PH

A group of Filipino students and advocates committed to raising awareness about flood vulnerability — and driving change through the lens of SDG 11.

🎯

Our Mission

To educate and empower Filipino communities with the knowledge to understand, advocate for, and act on flood resilience under SDG 11.

👁

Our Vision

A Philippines where every city and community is prepared, protected, and heard — where floods no longer mean helplessness.

🌊

Our Focus

SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities and Communities — as the framework for understanding and addressing the root causes of flooding in the Philippines.

Our Story

Born from Frustration, Built on Hope

BahaTayo PH started as a school advocacy project — but quickly became something bigger. We were frustrated watching our communities flood year after year, hearing the same promises, and seeing the same neglect.

We realized that most Filipinos understand floods as inevitable. But floods are not just natural events — they are the result of policy choices, land use decisions, and years of under-investment in urban resilience. We built this platform to change that narrative.

The name BahaTayo is a play on words — "baha" (flood) and "tayo" (us / we rise) — because we believe communities don't just endure floods. They rise from them.
